Chamber Site Plan

Overview

Site Name

Ensenada Commerce Chamber

Site Name Reason

It's a site for businesses in Ensenada, the site name of Ensenada Commerce Chamber seemed fitting.

Site Purpose

Show the visitor information about different restaurants to eat at and a variety of places to go see in Baja California.

Scenarios

  • What places are there to see in Baja California?
  • What is there to eat in Baja California?
