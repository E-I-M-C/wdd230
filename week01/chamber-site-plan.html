Chamber Site Plan

Overview

Site Name

Ensenada Chamber of Commerce

Site Name Reason

It's a site for businesses in Ensenada, the site name of "Ensenada Chamber of Commerce" seemed fitting.

Site Purpose

Gather local businesses of Ensenda and help them collaborate together to advance their goals and interests that are in common and run various activities that are helpful such as hosting a networking event.

Scenarios

  • What's the number of businesses that have joined this chamber?
  • What resources will be provided by this chamber?
  • How well does this chamber communicate with its customers?
